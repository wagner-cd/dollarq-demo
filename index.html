About Enhanced DollarQ Recognition

This demo uses an advanced implementation of the $Q Super-Quick Recognizer with 16 sophisticated gesture templates including various ellipse ratios, comprehensive directional lines (6 orientations), and geometric shapes. The enhanced version features improved recognition algorithms, better confidence scoring, and specialized templates for precise shape detection.

Advanced Templates: 16 sophisticated gesture patterns including ultra-precise ellipses and 6-orientation line system
Enhanced Algorithms: Improved point cloud processing and lookup table optimization
Professional Confidence: Sophisticated scoring system for reliable recognition
Geometric Precision: Specialized templates for various aspect ratios (1:1, 7:1, 15:1)

Based on the research by Vatavu, R.-D., Anthony, L. and Wobbrock, J.O. (2018). $Q: A super-quick, articulation-invariant stroke-gesture recognizer for low-resource devices.
